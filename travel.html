Looking for a cool holiday? Here are some great places to travel...

Hotel de Glace, Quebec

Hotel de Glace in Quebec is currently the only ice hotel in North America. It was first built in 2001, and is made of 15,000 tons of snow and 500,000 tons of ice.

Snow Village, Finland

Every year in late November, 1,000 trucks of snow are used to create Finland's Snow Village.
